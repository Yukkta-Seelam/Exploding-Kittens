    How to Play Exploding Kittens

    Goal: Be the last player standing! Avoid drawing an Exploding Kitten.

    On your turn: Play any cards you want (or none), then draw a card to end your turn.

    Card Types

    • Exploding Kitten — If you draw this without a Defuse, you're out!
    • Defuse — Use when you draw an Exploding Kitten to survive. Place the Kitten back anywhere in the deck.
    • Skip — End your turn without drawing.
    • Attack — End your turn; next player draws 2 cards (can stack!).
    • See the Future — Peek at the top 3 cards of the deck.
    • Shuffle — Shuffle the draw pile.
    • Nope — Cancel any action (except Exploding Kitten).
    • Favor — Force a player to give you a card of their choice.
    • Cat Cards — Play 2 matching cats to steal a random card; 3 matching to request a specific card.

    5 Unique Cards: Play 5 different cards to take any card from the discard pile.

    Player 1 Wins! 🎉

    They avoided all the Exploding Kittens!
